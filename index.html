How to use this Product Delivery OS

This dashboard is the shared operating system for building Fashion Genius Creator Studio. It explains what we are building, why we are building it, what is active now, and what is intentionally parked.

1. Start with the Strategic Briefing

Understand the current product focus before starting new work: creator adoption, monetization, mobile web usability and PMF validation.

2. Work from the Sprint Board

Use the sprint board to see current priorities, owners, status, estimates and QA stage. Do not start parked items without approval.

3. Follow the Quality Gate

Every feature must move through development, QA, bug review and release readiness before it is considered done.

4. Capture ideas in the Innovation Vault

Future ideas are welcome. They are documented, not lost. But they stay parked until the required milestone is reached.
